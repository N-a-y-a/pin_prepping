N-a-y-a Pin Prepping

Effortless Pinterest scheduling for creators

About

N-a-y-a is a personal project designed to help users prepare and schedule Pinterest pins in advance. Whether you're a solo creator or sharing content with friends and family, N-a-y-a Pin Prepping makes it easy to stay consistent without daily posting.

Privacy Policy

This app uses the Pinterest API to enable pin scheduling. We do not collect or share personal data. Your Pinterest account information is used solely to provide app functionality and is never stored or sold.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
